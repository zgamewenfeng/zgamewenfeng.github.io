Line_Match

This is an interesting casual game, with simple line connection and exciting elimination, challenging your thoughts again and again!

To truly excel in this game, you need to carefully plan every move, and only through careful consideration can your gaming journey be longer and give you a real chance to top the high score board.
Simple operation doesn't mean the game content is superficial. Whether you're waiting for the bus, riding the subway, or in any leisure time, you can start a game at any time and enjoy a moment of joy. More delightfully, the game will thoughtfully record your progress every time, so whenever you reopen it, you can continue the challenge from where you left off, without worrying that your previous efforts will be wasted.
Let's review the exciting gameplay of this game:
Skillfully connect numbers in a straight line that are the same or add up to ten, and they will magically disappear.
Your move times will be displayed at the bottom of the game interface, and whenever they reach zero, a new row of numbers will appear on the screen.
If no eliminable numbers can be found on the entire game interface, a new row of numbers will be added to the game.
When the numbers touch the top of the screen, it means your game journey comes to an end.
Don't forget to use those magical props when you're in trouble; they will give you a helping hand.
Every time you successfully eliminate a row of numbers, your score will increase. After the game ends, these scores can be converted into gold coins, storing energy for your future challenges.
Climb to the top of the leaderboard, challenge your limits, and see how far you can go!
